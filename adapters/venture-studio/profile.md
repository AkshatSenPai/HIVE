# Business Profile — Venture Studio

## What the business is
An indie product studio: find underserved app/site opportunities, build small
focused products fast, ship them (App Store / Play Store / web), and iterate
or kill based on traction.

## Offer
Small consumer and prosumer utilities: single-problem apps, niche SaaS,
content-adjacent tools. Bias to things one person plus agents can ship in
weeks, not quarters.

## ICP (who we build for)
Underserved niches with visible demand signals: recurring complaints in
reviews/forums, "I'd pay for X" threads, tools with bad UX and captive users,
platform gaps after policy changes.

## Positioning
Speed and volume with a quality floor: many small bets, each cheap to try,
killed fast if flat. Revenue models: paid upfront, subscription, or ads —
decided per product before build starts.

## Voice
Internal documents: blunt, evidence-first, numbers over adjectives. Every
opportunity claim needs a demand signal attached, not a vibe.
